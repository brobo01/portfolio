BEN ROBERTS

JUNIOR SOFTWARE DEVELOPER

 Hi, I'm Ben. I'm a huge fan of start-ups and it doesn't matter where you find me, I'll probably be talking about how start-ups change the world.

I recently graduated from General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive course, a three-month coding bootcamp with a view to changing careers and getting in to the fascinating world of tech.

The challenges, opportunities to problem solve and to implement solutions in really float my boat.

Speaking of boats - my personal interests. If it moves and can be driven, I'm interested in it. Even better if you can take friends and ends with having a beer.

I've spent the last 8 years in investment banking and corporate finance, helping companies raise primary and secondary funds from the public markets and complete M&A. However, I've always been more interested in the businesses themselves, which is why I've made the jump to start a career in.

As a corporate-financier turned developer, I look to bring together what I learnt at General Assembly with the skills and knowledge amassed from 8 years of corporate finance, along with my work ethic and team working ability, and apply them to technology start-ups.

If you want to talk about coding, business or things that move and can be driven, hit me up and hopefully it'll end with having a beer.
